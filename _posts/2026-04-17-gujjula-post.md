Congratulations to [Vinay Gujjula](https://rokitalab.com/members/13-vinay-gujjula.html) for being awarded a [Pediatric Oncology Student Training (POST) Award](https://www.alexslemonade.org/where-money-goes/research/post-recipients) from Alex's Lemonade Stand Foundation (ALSF). Vinay's project "Investigating the association of genetic ancestry with pathogenic variation in pediatric patients with brain tumors" will support the lab's germline variant analysis and broader pediatric brain tumor research through ancestry-informed computational analyses of large pediatric cancer datasets.

We commend Vinay on this well-deserved recognition, and thank Alex's Lemonade Stand Foundation for continuing to support the next generation of pediatric cancer researchers!
