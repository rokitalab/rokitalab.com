Congratulations to [Dr. Patricia Sullivan](https://rokitalab.com/labmembers/03-patricia-sullivan.html) on being awarded a prestigious [Young Investigator Grant](https://www.alexslemonade.org/grant-categories/young-investigator) from Alex’s Lemonade Stand Foundation (ALSF)!

This award, entitled **“Decoding splicing dysregulation in pediatric CNS tumors through multi-omic integration,”** will support Patricia’s research for three years and expand our lab’s efforts to understand how RNA splicing contributes to pediatric brain tumor biology. 
Through this work, Patricia will integrate transcriptomic and multi-omic data to identify splicing alterations that may reveal new tumor dependencies and therapeutic opportunities.

We are thrilled to celebrate this well-deserved recognition of Patricia’s exciting research program. Congratulations, Patricia, and a warm thank you to Alex’s Lemonade Stand Foundation for supporting this important work!
